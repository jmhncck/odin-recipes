Bread

A beautiful rustic loaf made easily in your home oven.

This no knead recipe only takes minutes to get a dough started. Let time and fermentatioh do the rest of the work.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/4 tsp instant yeast
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cup water

Recipe

  1. Mix dry goods together in a large bowl, add warm water, and mix
  2. Cover bowl and let dough sit in warm area for 12-18 hours
  3. Fold dough inwards from four different sides, then recover, and let sit for 15 minutes
  4. Shape dough into a ball, cover and let sit for two hours
  5. Preheat oven to 450F and leave dutch oven inside to heat up for 30 minutes
  6. Bake bread for 30 minutes covered, 15 minutes uncovered
