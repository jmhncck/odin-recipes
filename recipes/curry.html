Red Curry

An amazingly light and flavorfull meal that can be ready in minutes.

Go to your local asian grocery store and pick up curry paste and coconut milk. It's really that simple

Ingredients

  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp curry paste
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 pounds chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup broccoli
  • 1/2 cup eggplant
  • 1 cup rice
  • sesame seeds
  • green onion

Recipe

  1. Boil 2 cups of water, add rice, cover, and cook for 10 minutes
