Red Curry

An amazingly light and flavorfull meal that can be ready in minutes.

Go to your local asian grocery store and pick up curry paste and coconut milk. It's really that simple

Ingredients

  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp curry paste
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 pounds chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup broccoli
  • 1/2 cup eggplant
  • 1 cup rice
  • sesame seeds
  • green onion

Recipe

  1. Slice broccoli and eggplant into thin, bitesize pieces
  2. Boil 2 cups of water, add rice, cover, and cook for 10 minutes
  3. Heat coconut milk in pan over medium heat
  4. Add curry paste, salt, chicken, and veggies
  5. Cook for 10-12 minutes until chicken is fully cooked
  6. Serve with rice and add sesame seeds, and green onion as garnish
