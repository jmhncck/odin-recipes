Cauliflower Shwarma

A colorful vegetarian dish using a mix of spices, oil, and tahini sauce.

Cover your vegetables in oil and spices, roast, and serve along side fresh cut cucumbers, tomatoes, and top it all with a wonderful tahini sauce made with lemon, garlic, water and tahini paste

Ingredients

  • 1 cauliflower head
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1/2 tsp tumeric
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 5 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small cucumber
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/3 cup tahini paste
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • pita bread to serve

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 425F
  2. Cut onion into small wedges
  3. Cut cauliflower to bitesize pieces
  4. Mix spices with oil
  5. Toss cauliflower and onion in bowl with spices
  6. Bake cauliflower and onion in oven for 30-40 minutes
  7. Combine tahini paste, lemon juice, garlic, and 5 tbsp water
  8. Toast pita bread
  9. Make a pita sandwich with your roasted cauliflower, onions, cucumber, tomato, and tahini sauce
